Frequently Ask Questions

Ordering and Shipping

How long does it take to receive my order?

The delivery time depends on your location and the product availability. Generally, orders are delivered within 3-5 business days.

What is the shipping cost?

We offer free delivery for all orders.

Do you ship internationally?

At the moment, we only ship within the country.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account on our website and checking the order status.

Cancellations and Modifications

Can I cancel my order after it has been placed?

If your order has not been shipped, you can cancel it by contacting our customer support team. However, if it has already been shipped, you will need to refuse the delivery and wait for the item to be returned to us before we can process the cancellation.

Can I modify my order after it has been placed?

Once the order has been placed, you cannot modify it. You will need to cancel the order and place a new one with the updated information.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

You can return the product within 7 days of delivery if it is faulty or not as described. The product must be unused and in its original packaging.

How do I return a product?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team. We will guide you through the process and provide you with a return label.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive the returned product, we will process the refund within 3-5 business days. It may take additional time for the refund to reflect on your bank statement.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards and mobile wallets.

Is it safe to make payments on your website?

Yes, we use secure payment gateways to ensure the safety of your transactions.
